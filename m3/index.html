The BMW M5 is a modified BMW Motorsport division of the BMW automobile series. The first generation was introduced in 1986. The next generation M5 is interchangeable with all generations of cars, including E34, E39, E60/61, F10. The final production model is the G30, and after the first orders have been received, final production of the M version will take place for the first time in 2018.
